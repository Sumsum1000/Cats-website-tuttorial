The Yorkshire Terrier (often shortened as Yorkie) is one of the smallest dog breed of terrier type, and of any dog breed. The breed developed during the 19th century in Yorkshire, England.[1] Ideally its maximum size is 7 pounds (3.2 kg).[2] A popular companion dog, the Yorkshire Terrier has also been part of the development of other breeds, such as the Silky Terrier. It has a grey, black and tan coat.
